(i)  DD (CIM) program consists of 381 credits including 36 credits for 6 electives for the B.Tech. part, 30 credits for honors,

(iv) For 6 B.Tech. electives each student must select 6 courses from the elective list III, IV, VI to IX given below.

 

(v) Note the instructions given below.
